Section 2
Our Commitments to Each Other
These are the behaviours we commit to as a team — not rules imposed from outside, but promises we make to each other.
01
We communicate openly and early.
We flag problems before they become crises. We don't let things fester.
02
We show up prepared.
Meetings have agendas. We read what needs to be read. We respect each other's time.
03
We follow up. Always.
A request answered quickly is better than a perfect answer delivered late.
04
We give feedback directly — and kindly.
We don't talk around the person. We address issues with the person involved, not about them.
05
We back each other in front of customers.
Disagreements stay internal. Outside, we are one team.
06
We celebrate wins — even the small ones.
Progress deserves recognition. We say well done when well done is earned.
